Getting Started with LEAP Pro

Welcome to LEAP Pro

LEAP Pro (Landholder Environmental Action Planner) is a comprehensive tool designed to help Australian landholders create and implement environmental management plans. Whether you're managing a small rural property or a large agricultural holding, LEAP Pro guides you through the process of identifying issues, creating action plans, and accessing resources.

Tip: You can save your progress at any time - your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage.

Who Should Use This?

Landholders, farmers, Landcare groups, and environmental managers

Time Required

Complete in 2-4 hours or work incrementally over days

End Result

Professional PDF report with action plan

Key Features

Automated Recommendations

LEAP Pro analyzes your property assessment and automatically generates tailored environmental action recommendations. The system considers your location, identified issues, and management goals to provide practical, actionable advice.

Interactive Mapping

Create detailed maps of your property with multiple base layers (street, satellite, topographic). Draw zones, mark problem areas, and add photos with GPS coordinates. All mapping data is included in your final report.

Professional Reporting

Generate comprehensive PDF reports including all assessment data, maps, photos, action plans, budgets, and timeline. Reports are professionally formatted and suitable for council submissions or grant applications.

State-Specific Data

Access localized information including common weed species, funding programs, and regulatory requirements specific to your state or territory. This ensures your plan is relevant to local conditions.

Mapping Tools Guide

Using the Interactive Map

The mapping tool is one of LEAP Pro's most powerful features. Here's how to make the most of it:

Finding Your Property

Use the search button to enter your address or location. You can also use "My Location" to automatically zoom to your current position.

Drawing Zones

Use drawing tools (top-right of map) to create polygons or markers. Click to add points, double-click to finish.

Pro Tip: For accurate mapping, use the satellite view to identify features like creeks, vegetation boundaries, and erosion areas.

Adding Photos to Your Map

  1. Click "Upload Photos" in the mapping section
  2. Select images from your device (JPEG, PNG formats)
  3. Add descriptive notes for each photo
  4. Photos will be included in your final PDF report

Troubleshooting

Map not loading or showing errors

If the map isn't loading properly:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Try a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended)
  • Clear browser cache and try again
Data not saving properly

If your progress isn't being saved:

  • Ensure you're not using private/incognito mode
  • Check browser settings allow local storage
  • Try clicking "Save Draft" before leaving
  • Export a backup regularly using the Export panel
PDF generation issues

If PDF export fails:

  • Ensure you have permission to save files on your device
  • Try with fewer photos selected
  • Check available disk space
  • Use CSV export as an alternative

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally in your browser's storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server unless you explicitly choose to share it. You can clear all data at any time from Settings.

Can I work on multiple properties?

Currently, LEAP Pro stores data for one property at a time. To work on multiple properties, export your current plan, then use "Clear All Data" to start fresh with a new property.

Is internet required to use LEAP Pro?

An initial internet connection is required to load the application and mapping libraries. Once loaded, you can work offline, though some features like map searching require internet.

Can I edit my plan after exporting?

Yes. Simply continue working in LEAP Pro - your data remains in the application until you clear it. You can regenerate the PDF at any time with updated information.

Is LEAP Pro free to use?

Yes, LEAP Pro is completely free to use. It's designed to support Australian landholders in environmental management planning.
